## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are the inference limits included on each plan?">
    Free plans include \$30 in total inference allowance. Pro plans include up to \$1,500/month, subject to a daily limit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do credits work?">
    Credit top-ups are available on Pro plans. If your usage exceeds your daily allowance, credits keep your work from being interrupted.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I purchase more credits for my Pro organization?">
    Pro org admins can enable credit billing in Settings \> Credits. You can either buy a one-time credit top-up or set up automatic rules (a balance threshold that triggers a charge and the amount added each time). Once enabled, any usage beyond your daily allowance draws from this balance automatically. Organizations can hold up to \$50,000 in credits per month.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
