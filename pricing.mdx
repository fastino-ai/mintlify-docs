## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I purchase more credits for my organization?">
    Pro and Enterprise org admins can enable credit billing in Settings -\> Billing. You can either buy a one-time credit top-up or set up automatic rules (a balance threshold that triggers a charge and the amount added each time). Once enabled, any usage beyond your monthly allowance draws from this balance automatically. Organizations can hold up to \$50,000 in credits per month.
  </Accordion>
