## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are router credits?">
    Router credits are inference credits that apply to any request sent through Pioneer's model router. Pro includes \$40/seat/month in router credits. Enterprise includes \$50/seat/month. Credits are consumed based on the routed model's token usage at standard rates.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between router credits and usage credits?">
    Router credits only apply to traffic that goes through the model router and are included in both Pro and Enterprise plans.

    Usage credits work two ways: direct calls to a specific model (if you want to pin a model and bypass the router), and overflow once your monthly router credits run out. In both cases, you can top up usage credits to keep going.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I purchase more credits for my organization?">
    Pro and Enterprise org admins can enable credit billing in Settings -\> Billing. You can either buy a one-time credit top-up or set up automatic rules (a balance threshold that triggers a charge and the amount added each time). Once enabled, any usage beyond your monthly allowance draws from this balance automatically. Organizations can hold up to \$50,000 in credits per month.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  Credit top-ups are now available on Pro and Enterprise plans. Prompt caching can also help you save more on inference cost - see how caching works [here](https://docs.pioneer.ai/api-reference/prompt-caching).
</Note>
